Khedebneithirbinet I ("Neith kills the evil eye") was an ancient Egyptian queen from the 26th Dynasty, probably the wife of pharaoh Necho II and the mother of his successor, Psamtik II.


== Biography ==
The identification as Necho's wife is solely based on the fact that her sarcophagus dates to the 26th Dynasty, that her titles as King's wife and King's mother fit, and that no other wife is attested for the king.
