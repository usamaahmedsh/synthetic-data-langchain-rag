Intimate Portrait was a biographical documentary television series on the Lifetime cable network hosted by Meredith Vieira and focusing on different female celebrities, including stars from the fields of cinema, music, politics, sports and others which includes interviews with each subject and appearance's by numerous stars discussing the subject. 12 seasons were made with a total of 271 episodes, airing from January 3, 1994, and October 3, 2005. The series utilises stock footage, on-camera interviews, and photographs of the celebrities as children.
