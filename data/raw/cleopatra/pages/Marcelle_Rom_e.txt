Marcelle Romée (7 February 1903 – 3 December 1932) was a French stage and film actress. Having emerged as a film star, and played the female lead role in four films, she committed suicide by drowning in the River Seine in Paris. 


== Filmography ==
La lettre (1931)
Le cap perdu (1931)
The Night at the Hotel (1932)
Lilac (1932)
