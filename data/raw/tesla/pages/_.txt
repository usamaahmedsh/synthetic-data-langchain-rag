✕ (Unicode character U+2715 ✕ MULTIPLICATION X) may be used as:

An x mark, a mark used to for negation or indication
A multiplication sign (more exactly, U+00D7 × MULTIPLICATION SIGN), a rotationally symmetric saltire
The sign for cross product (more exactly, U+2A2F ⨯ VECTOR OR CROSS PRODUCT), a binary operation on two vectors in three-dimensional space
