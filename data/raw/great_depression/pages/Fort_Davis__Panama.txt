Fort William D. Davis is a former U.S. Army fort near Gatun, Panama. It was transferred to Panama in 1995.


== Current use ==
Panama renamed the location the José Dominador Bazán residential area. It is used for civilian housing, education and production facilities, including an established 400 seat international call center (Support Services Group) in operation since 2004. It is also the Panamanian National Police Training Academy grounds since 2014.
