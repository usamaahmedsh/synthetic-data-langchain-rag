WAtoday is an online newspaper, focusing its coverage on Perth and Western Australia. It was established on 10 June 2008 and is owned by Nine Entertainment (formerly Fairfax Digital).
The company employs ten journalists in Perth. It is based in the same building as Nine television, on St George's Terrace.
It is in competition with the online services provided by The West Australian and PerthNow, both owned by Seven West Media.
