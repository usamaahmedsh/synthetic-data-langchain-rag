Last Week Tonight with John Oliver is an American late-night talk show created and hosted by John Oliver for HBO. The show takes a satirical look at the week in news, politics and current events.
As of November 16, 2025, 350 episodes of Last Week Tonight with John Oliver have aired. The show's twelfth season premiered on February 16, 2025 with the finale airing on November 16 of the same year.
