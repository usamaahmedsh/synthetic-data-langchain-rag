CB Insights is a private company with a business analytics platform and global database that provides market intelligence on private companies and investor activities. The platform provides summaries of private companies to private equity, venture capital, investment banking, angel investing, and consulting professionals.


== Founding ==
CB Insights was founded on January 1, 2008, by Anand Sanwal, and Jonathan Sherry and is headquartered in New York. 
The company has raised capital in three rounds of funding with the most recent one being $10 million in series A funding raised in 2015, led by the growth stage investment firm RSTP. The company had previously raised $1.15 million in grants from the National Science Foundation for its private company scoring software, called Mosaic.


== Business ==
CB Insights uses a combination of big data tools and algorithms, as well as sentiment analysis on publicly available signals to gather and analyze data about private companies, investors, and industries.
The company's reports on the health of private companies, growth startups, and venture capital across industries have been published in work by The New York Times and PwC.
Since 2017, CB Insights publishes an annual rating of companies in artificial intelligence.
The company announced acquisition of VentureSource data assets from Dow Jones & Company on July 15, 2020. 
CB Insights' primary competitors include Crunchbase, and Owler.
