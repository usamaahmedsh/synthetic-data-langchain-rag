PRIMA is an interbank network operating in Indonesia and Singapore, owned by PT Rintis Sejahtera, a satellite communication service provider. Previously, it was known as the ATM BCA network, serving Bank Central Asia.


== Services ==
PRIMA provides various services, including Primamesh, Primalink, Primastar, Primanet, and EFT switching, which serves as the interbank network. The company also offers broadband services to support digital communication needs.
