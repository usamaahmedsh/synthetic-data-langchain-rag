3DD Productions is a UK-based television production company with its own international licensing company. 3DD Entertainment together forming the 3DD Group.
Now in its 30th year beginning life as a sales company it now produces all its own programmes now reaching 1000, the company's owners and team focus on history, music, culture, cinema, music, War and hosted series.
