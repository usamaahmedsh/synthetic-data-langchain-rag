Gnome-Pie is a circular application launcher for Linux created by Simon Schneegans.
It is made of several pies, each consisting of multiple slices. The user presses a keystroke, which opens the desired pie. By activating one of its slices, applications may be launched, key presses may be simulated or files can be opened.
