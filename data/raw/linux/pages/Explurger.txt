Explurger is a travel social media app, it helps users to share their real time experiences and discovering new places through user-generated content. 
The app integrates social media and location-based services, enabling users to share check-ins, reviews, photos and videos, while also creating an automated travelogue from their journeys.


== History ==
Explurger was launched in 2022, with the idea of creating a platform that encourages exploration and real-time sharing.  Jitin Bhatia created Explurger app, inspired by his desire to recall the places he had visited, which led to the creation of a digital travelogue. Jitin Bhatia and actor Sonu Sood later collaborated on a gamified travel platform, combining Bhatia's idea for a gamified travel platform with Sood's commitment to community-building. Explurger was officially launched in 2022 after spending over seven years developing it. 
Explurger has over 20 million downloads across iOS and Android, with users spending an average of 17 minutes on the app. It is now used in more than 75 countries.
The app was created to cater to users who are interested in discovering new places, events, and experiences while sharing insights with a community. In 2021, the app company received a $1 million investment round from numerous investors. In 2024, Explurger raised $4.5 million funding round led by Affle India.


== Reception ==
Explurger has received generally positive reviews from users, particularly for its user-friendly interface and emphasis on location-based discovery. It has been praised for its ability to combine social networking with practical features for urban exploration and travel.
