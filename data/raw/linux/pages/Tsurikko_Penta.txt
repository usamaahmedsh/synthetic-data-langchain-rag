Tsurikko Penta (つりっ子ペン太, Penta's Fisherman) is a fishing-themed medal game based on the Antarctic Adventure series released by Konami in 1991 in Japan along with Ballon Penta and Imo Hori Penta. It was also made as a Redemption game named Super Fisherman Penta and the mobile-based game Penta no Tsuri Bōken, which was available only on Konami's now defunct KonamiNet DX service. All of these were released in Japan only as well.
