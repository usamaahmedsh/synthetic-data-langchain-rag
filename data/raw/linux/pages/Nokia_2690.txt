The Nokia 2690 is a mobile phone released by Nokia in March 2010. It operates on GSM quad band frequency 850, 900, 1800 and 1900 MHz, with automatic switching between frequencies. It has dimensions of 107.5 × 45.5 × 13.8 mm and weighs 80.7 grams (2.85 oz).


== Technical specifications ==
The Nokia 2690 has a 1.8 inches (4.6 cm) screen and a digital camera that takes photos (640 × 480 resolution) and videos (176 × 144 resolution). It also includes an FM radio. It can connect to the Internet using the Opera Mini browser.
