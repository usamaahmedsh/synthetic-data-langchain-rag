Riotsville, U.S.A. is a 2022 American documentary film by Sierra Pettengill and narrated by Charlene Modeste.


== Summary ==
Using mainly archival footage shot by the media and U.S. government, the film examines fictional towns (which the filmmaker discovered after reading author Rick Perlstein's 2008 book Nixonland) to combat rioters that were created by military officials during the civil unrest of 1960s America.


== Reception ==
The film received 90% on Rotten Tomatoes from critics.
