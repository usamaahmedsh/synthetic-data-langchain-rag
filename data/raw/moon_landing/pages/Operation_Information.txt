Operation Information is a DuMont Television Network public affairs TV show giving veterans information on their rights and benefits. The show aired Thursdays from July 17, 1952, to September 18, 1952. DuMont had previously aired a similar series for veterans Operation Success (1948â€“49).

As with many DuMont series, no episodes are known to exist.
