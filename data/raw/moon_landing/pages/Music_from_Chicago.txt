Music from Chicago is a program broadcast on the DuMont Television Network from WGN-TV in Chicago. The show first aired April 15, 1951, and the last show aired June 17, 1951. It was broadcast on Sundays from 9:30 to 10 p.m. Eastern Time. 


== Episode status ==
As with most DuMont series, no episodes are known to exist.
