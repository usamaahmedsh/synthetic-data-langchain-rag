Nine Thirty Curtain is a dramatic anthology television series which aired on the DuMont Television Network from October 16, 1953, to January 1, 1954. The 30-minute show aired on Fridays at 9:30 pm ET.


== Episode status ==
As with most DuMont series, no episodes are known to survive.
