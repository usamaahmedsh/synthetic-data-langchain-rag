Hogar TV (Arabic: الهقار تي في) is an Arabic language satellite television channel broadcasting from hydra. Hogar TV was set up by an Algerian businessman Mohammed Mouloudi with a number of Arab intellectuals from Algeria and the Arab World.


== History ==
Hogar TV was founded in May 2012, it has started to broadcast its programs in May 2012.
