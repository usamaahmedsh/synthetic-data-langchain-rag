Territorial Formula Financing (TFF) is an annual unconditional transfer payment from Canada's federal government to the three territorial governments of Yukon, the Northwest Territories, and Nunavut to support the provision of public services.
A significant portion of the financial resources of the territorial governments comes from the Canadian federal government through the TFF grant. In the 2023-24 fiscal year, Yukon will receive $1.252 billion ($28,208 per capita), the Northwest Territories $1.611 billion ($37,073 per capita) and Nunavut $1.971 billion ($47,520 per capita) for a total of $4.18 billion.
