The 2025 Thiruvananthapuram Municipal Corporation election is a municipal election to the Thiruvananthapuram Municipal Corporation which governs Thiruvananthapuram city in Kerala. It will be held on 9 December 2025.


== Election schedule ==
The election schedule was announced by the state election commission on 10 November 2025.
