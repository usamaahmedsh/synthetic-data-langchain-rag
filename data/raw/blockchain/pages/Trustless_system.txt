Trustless system is a, usually cryptographic, system where all involved stakeholders are not required to trust none of each other so allowing participants to engage in more precisely audited transactions. One of its most important technologies are immutable ledgers.


== Uses ==
It is specially useful for:

Information technology audit transparency.
Blockchain proof of stake.
Consensus algorithms.
Decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) zero trust architecture (ZTA).
Decentralized finance (DeFi) financial technology (fintech).
Smart contracts proof of work.
