Saminaka is a town and headquarters of Lere Local Government Area and of Saminaka chiefdom of the Akurmi people in southern Kaduna state in the Middle Belt region of Nigeria. The postal code of the area is 811.


== Population of Saminaka ==
The area has an altitude of about 869  feet or 264  meters and a population of about  4,917


== Climate condition ==
The weather condition of Saminaka is relatively temperate by day and cold by nights.
