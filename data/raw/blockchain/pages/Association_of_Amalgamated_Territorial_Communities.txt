The Association of Amalgamated Territorial Communities (AACT, Ukrainian: Всеукраїнська Асоціація об’єднаних територіальних громад, romanized: Vseukrayinsʹka Asotsiatsiya ob'yednanykh terytorialʹnykh hromad, lit. 'All-Ukrainian Association of United Territorial Communities') is an organization of municipal government representing over 490 Ukrainian amalgamated  municipalities. Its stated purpose is to provide political and strategic leadership to represent the interests of local municipalities established in the process of voluntary association of territorial communities after Euromaidan. The AACT was founded in 2016.
