Malak Fakhar Zaman Khan Bangash (Urdu: فخر زمان خان) is a Pakistani politician who had been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan from February 2021 till July 2022.


== Political career ==
He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf from Constituency NA-45 (Tribal Area-VI) in a 2021 by-election in the Constituency.
