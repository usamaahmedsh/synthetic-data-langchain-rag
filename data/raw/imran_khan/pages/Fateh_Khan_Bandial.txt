Fateh Khan Bandial was a Pakistani career civil servant who served in BS-22 grade as the Federal Secretary for Interior. After his retirement, he also held the office of Federal Interior Minister of Pakistan since 23 July 1993 to 19 October 1993. He was the father of Justice Umar Ata Bandial. He had also served as Deputy Commissioner of Lahore.


== Death ==
He died on 9 November 2008 and received a eulogy from Prime Minister Yousuf Raza Gilani.
