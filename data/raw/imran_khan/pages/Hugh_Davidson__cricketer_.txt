Hugh Davidson (17 May 1907 – 22 April 1960) was an Australian cricketer. 


== Professional career ==
He played eleven first-class matches for New South Wales between 1927/28 and 1930/31.


== Personal life ==
His sister was artist Nancy Kilgour (1904–1954).
