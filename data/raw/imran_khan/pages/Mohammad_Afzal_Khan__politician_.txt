Mohammad Afzal Khan / Dr. Muhammad Afzal Khan Dhandla (ڈاکٹر محمد افضل دھاندلا) is a Pakistani politician who has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan since August 2018.


== Political career ==
He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan from Constituency NA-98 (Bhakkar-II) as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf in 2018 Pakistani general election.
