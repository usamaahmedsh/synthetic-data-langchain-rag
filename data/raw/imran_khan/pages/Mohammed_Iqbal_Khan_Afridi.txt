Mohammed Iqbal Khan (Urdu: محمد اقبال خان آفریدی) is a Pakistani politician who has been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan since February 2024. He previously served as a member from August 2013 to till 20 January 2023.


== Political career ==
He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) from Constituency NA-44 (Tribal Area-V) in the 2018 Pakistani general election. He received 12,537 votes and defeated Hameed Ullah Jan Afridi.
