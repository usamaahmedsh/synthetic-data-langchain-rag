Aftab Hussain Siddiqui (Urdu: آفتاب حسین صدیقی) is a Pakistani politician who had been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan from October 2018 till January 2023. He had secured a seat vacant by incumbent President of Pakistan, Arif Alvi.


== Political career ==
He was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan from Constituency NA-247 (Karachi South-II) as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf in a by-election held on 21 October 2018.
On 18 May 2023, he left the PTI due to the 2023 Pakistani protests.
