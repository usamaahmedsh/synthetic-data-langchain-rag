Dr. Muhammad Humayun Mohmand (Pashto: محمد ہمایوں مہمند) is a Pakistani politician who is currently serving as a member of the Senate of Pakistan from the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa since March 2021. He belongs to Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf.


== Political career ==
He was elected to the Senate of Pakistan on a technocrat seat from Khyber Pakhtunkhwa as a candidate of the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) in the 2021 Pakistani Senate election.
