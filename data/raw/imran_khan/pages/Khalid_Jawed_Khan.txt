Khalid Jawed Khan (Urdu: خالد جاوید خان) is a Pakistani lawyer who served as the 35th Attorney General of Pakistan. Khan is a Barrister at Law and Advocate of Supreme Court and has been practicing law since 1996. He specializes in Constitutional Law, Taxation, Service and Civil matters.


== Education ==
Khan got his L.L.B degree from Queen Mary, University of London, his B.C.L. (Bachelor of Civil Law from Hertford College, Oxford University, and his L.L.M from Harvard Law School. He was also called to Bar at Lincoln's Inn, London.
