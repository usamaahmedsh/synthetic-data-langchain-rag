Sadiq Mohammad (Urdu: صادق محمد, born 3 May 1945) is a Pakistani former Test cricketer and younger brother of the Pakistani batsmen Hanif and Mushtaq Mohammad. He made his Test debut in the first Test between Pakistan and New Zealand in 1969. The 4th Test against the West Indies in 1981 was his final Test for Pakistan. He played county cricket for Gloucestershire. Sadiq also coached the Pakistan cricket team to bronze at the 2010 Asian Games. He umpired in one ODI game in 2000.
As a child, he attended the Church Mission School (CMS) in Karachi.
