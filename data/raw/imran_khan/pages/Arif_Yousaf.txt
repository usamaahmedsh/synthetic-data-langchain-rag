Arif Yousaf (Pashto: عارف يوسف) is a Pakistani politician hailing from Peshawar, who served as a member in the 10th Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Assembly, belonging to the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf.


== Political career ==
Yousaf was elected as the member of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Assembly on ticket of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf from PK-04 (Peshawar-IV) in the 2013 Pakistani general election.
