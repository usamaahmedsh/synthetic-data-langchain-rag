Seemi Bokhari (Urdu: سیمی بخاری) is a Pakistani politician who had been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan from August 2018 till January 2023.


== Political career ==
She was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) on a reserved seat for women from Punjab in the 2018 Pakistani general election.


=== Resignation ===
In April 2022, she resigned from the National Assembly seat along with all the PTI members after the no-confidence motion against Imran Khan.
