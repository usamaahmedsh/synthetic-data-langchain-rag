Robert Warley Wilson (born 15 July 1934) is a former first-class cricketer who played for Oxford University in 1956 and 1957.


== Education ==
Wilson was born in Warley, Worcestershire, and educated at Warwick School before going up to Brasenose College, Oxford. 


== Career ==
An off-spin bowler, he played one match for Oxford University in 1956 and a full season in 1957. His best first-class figures were 4 for 42 against D. R. Jardine's XI in 1957.
After graduating from Oxford he went to Canada, where he played several matches of non-first-class cricket for the national team between 1959 and 1968.
