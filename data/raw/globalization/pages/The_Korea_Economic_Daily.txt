The Korea Economic Daily (Korean: 한국경제신문), nicknamed Hankyung (한경), is a conservative and business daily newspaper in South Korea. It is the largest business newspaper by revenue in South Korea. It was founded on October 12, 1964, as the Daily Economic Newspaper and took its current name in 1980. One of its subsidiaries is TenAsia, an entertainment news source.
