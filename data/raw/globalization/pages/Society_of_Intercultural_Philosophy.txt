Society of Intercultural Philosophy (SIP) (German: Gesellschaft für Interkulturelle Philosophie (GIP)) is a non-profit association dealing with intercultural philosophy based in Cologne. It was founded in 1992 and now has members all over the world. Claudia Bickmann and Georg Stenger are the presidents of the society.
