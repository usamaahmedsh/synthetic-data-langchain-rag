Abdol Majid Mirza Eyn-ed-Dowleh (Persian: عبدالمجیدمیرزا عین‌الدوله; 1845 – 2 November 1927) was a Qajar prince and twice Prime Minister of Iran. He was the eldest son of Prince Soltan-Ahmad Mirza Azod od-Dowleh and a grandson of Fat'h-Ali Shah Qajar.
Ein ad-Dowleh Mansion in Tehran belonged to him.
