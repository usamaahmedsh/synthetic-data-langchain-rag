Bienvillers-au-Bois (French pronunciation: [bjɛ̃vile o bwa]; Picard: Binvilèr-au-Bos) is a commune in the Pas-de-Calais department in the Hauts-de-France region in northern France.


== Geography ==
A farming and light industrial village located 11 miles (18 km) southwest of Arras at the junction of the D2, D8 and D62.
