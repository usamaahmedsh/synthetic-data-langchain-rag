The 1783 North Carolina gubernatorial election was held in April 1783 in order to elect the Governor of North Carolina. Incumbent Governor Alexander Martin was re-elected by the North Carolina General Assembly as he ran unopposed. The exact number of votes cast in this election is unknown.


== General election ==
On election day in April 1783, incumbent Governor Alexander Martin was re-elected by the North Carolina General Assembly. Martin was sworn in for his second term on April 30, 1783.
