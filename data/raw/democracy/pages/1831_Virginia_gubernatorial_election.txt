The 1831 Virginia gubernatorial election was held on February 12, 1831, in order to elect the Governor of Virginia. Incumbent Democratic Governor of Virginia John Floyd won re-election in the Virginia General Assembly as he ran unopposed.


== General election ==
On election day, February 12, 1831, Democratic nominee John Floyd easily won re-election as he ran unopposed. Floyd was sworn in for his second term on March 4, 1831.
