The 1783 Maryland gubernatorial election was held on November 22, 1783, in order to elect the Governor of Maryland. Incumbent Governor William Paca was easily re-elected by the Maryland General Assembly as he ran unopposed. The exact results of this election are unknown.


== General election ==
On election day, November 22, 1783, William Paca was re-elected by the Maryland General Assembly. Paca was sworn in for his second term on November 22, 1783.
