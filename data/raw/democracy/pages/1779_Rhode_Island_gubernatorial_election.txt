The 1779 Rhode Island gubernatorial election was held on April 7, 1779, in order to elect the governor of Rhode Island.  Incumbent Independent governor William Greene won re-election as he ran unopposed. The exact number of votes cast in this election are unknown.


== General election ==
On election day, April 7, 1779, incumbent Independent governor William Greene won re-election as he ran unopposed, thereby retaining Independent control over the office of governor. Greene was sworn in for his second term on May 5, 1779.
