The 1810 Rhode Island gubernatorial election was held on April 4, 1810, in order to elect the governor of Rhode Island.  Incumbent Democratic-Republican governor James Fenner won re-election as he ran unopposed. The exact number of votes cast in this election are unknown.


== General election ==
On election day, April 4, 1810, incumbent Democratic-Republican governor James Fenner won re-election as he ran unopposed, thereby retaining Democratic-Republican control over the office of governor. Fenner was sworn in for his fourth term on May 1, 1810.
