The 1794 Rhode Island gubernatorial election was held on April 2, 1794, in order to elect the governor of Rhode Island.  Incumbent Democratic-Republican governor Arthur Fenner won re-election as he ran unopposed. The exact number of votes cast in this election are unknown.


== General election ==
On election day, April 2, 1794, incumbent Democratic-Republican governor Arthur Fenner won re-election as he ran unopposed, thereby gaining Democratic-Republican control over the office of governor. Fenner was sworn in for his fifth term on May 5, 1794.
