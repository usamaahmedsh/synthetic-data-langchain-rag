The 1783 Rhode Island gubernatorial election was held on April 2, 1783, in order to elect the governor of Rhode Island.  Incumbent Independent governor William Greene won re-election as he ran unopposed. The exact number of votes cast in this election are unknown.


== General election ==
On election day, April 2, 1783, incumbent Independent governor William Greene won re-election as he ran unopposed, thereby retaining Independent control over the office of governor. Greene was sworn in for his sixth term on May 5, 1783.
