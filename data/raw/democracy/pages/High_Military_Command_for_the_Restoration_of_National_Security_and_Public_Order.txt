The High Military Command for the Restoration of National Security and Public Order (Portuguese: Alto Comando Militar para a Restauração da Segurança Nacional e Ordem Pública) is the current military junta of Guinea-Bissau, announced on 26 November 2025 by its Spokesperson (Head), Brigadier General Dinis Incanha, during the 2025 coup d'état. After claiming control, they ordered the closure of all borders, established an overnight curfew, and ordered the suspension of the electoral process until further notice.
On 27 November 2025, Incanha presided over the investiture ceremony of General Horta Inta-A Na Man as Transitional President of Guinea-Bissau and Head of the High Military Command for the Restoration of National Security and Public Order.


== Heads ==
