He lived in Gran Municipality in Hadeland, Norway. Prior to his death, he had hosted a number of illegal gatherings. Gaarder had previously been involved in conspiracy theories related to swine flu and the Illuminati.
He is believed to have been one of the creators of the Norwegian online alternative-news magazine "Nyhetsspeilet" ("The News Mirror").
