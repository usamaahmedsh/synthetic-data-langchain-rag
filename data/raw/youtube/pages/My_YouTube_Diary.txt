My YouTube Diary (Korean: 조아서 구독중) is a South Korean web series. It aired on cable network Tooniverse and YouTube channel at 5:00 and 8:00 every Friday on August 9 to November 15, 2019, in the first season. A sequel titled My Mukbang Diary (조아서 먹방중) was premiered on August 7 to September 25, 2020. The second season aired from October 23 to December 11, 2020.
