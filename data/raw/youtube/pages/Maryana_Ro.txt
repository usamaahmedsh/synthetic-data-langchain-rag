Maryana Andreyevna Rozhkova (Russian: Марьяна Андреевна Рожкова; born October 7, 1999, Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk), better known as Maryana Ro (Russian: Марьяна Ро) is a Russian video blogger, singer and actress. Maryana Ro became an idol of teenagers in the 2010s, along with such video bloggers as Roma Zholud and EeOneGuy.


== Biography ==
She was born on October 7, 1999, in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk. She has a younger sister Maresya and an older brother Miroslav.
Since the age of 15, she has been in a close relationship with 19-year-old video blogger EeOneGuy. In December 2016, it became known that the couple broke up.
In September 2017, it became known about a close relationship between Maryana Rozhkova and rap singer FACE.
In July 2020, she came out as pansexual. In 2020, Maryana revealed that Ivan Dremin is her legal husband.
Since January 2022, Maryana Ro has been living in Japan.


== Career ==
Since 2013, she has been a video blogger on YouTube. Many attribute Rozhkova's rise in popularity to her close relationship with video blogger EeOneGuy.
On September 19, 2015, she released her first music single, thus launching her music career.
Maryana's musical career has often been compared to that of Roma Zholudy, who also gained popularity at the age of 15 on Russian side of YouTube.
