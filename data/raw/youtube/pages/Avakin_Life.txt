Avakin Life is a 3D life simulation computer and mobile video game developed and published by Lockwood Publishing, a company based in Nottingham, England. The game was first released in December 2013 for Android devices. As per 2022, it has more than 200 million registered users on iOS, Android and ChromeOS and more than a million daily players.
According to the developer, Avakin Life was inspired by PlayStation Home, as a similar platform for mobile, and saw an increase in its player base after PlayStation Home's closure in 2015.
