Milos Rajković (Serbian Cyrillic: Милош Рајковић; born 1985 in Belgrade) is a Serbian animator known by the pseudonym Sholim.
Sholim is known for his surreal looped GIFs and animations, most of his videos are made with Photoshop and After Effects.
