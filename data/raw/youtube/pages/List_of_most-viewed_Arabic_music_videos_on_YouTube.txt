YouTube is an American video-sharing website headquartered in San Bruno, California.
"Lm3allem" by Moroccan singer Saad Lamjarred is the most-viewed Arabic music video and the first to reach 1 billion views in May 2023. "Ya Lili" by Tunisian singer Balti with Hammouda is the second video to garner over 700 million views. "Happy Happy" by Bahrani singer Hala Al Turk become the first Arabic music video to cross 100 million views. This is a list of the most-viewed Arabic music videos on YouTube.
