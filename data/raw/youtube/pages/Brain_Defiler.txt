Brain Defiler (Korean: 뇌절자; RR: Noejeolja),  also known as Brain Breaker and Brainwrecker is a South Korean variety web show hosted by South Korean singer-songwriter, K-pop idol, actor, presenter and television personality Kim Hee-chul. New episodes air every Wednesday at 6 pm KST on YouTube.
