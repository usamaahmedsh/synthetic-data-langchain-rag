YouTube is an American video-sharing website headquartered in San Bruno, California. 
In 2012, "Je m'appelle Funny Bear" by German virtual singer Gummibär became the first French-language music video to reach 100 million views.
In 2023, Indila's song "Dernière Danse" became the first music video in French to reach 1 billion views.

== Top song by a French artist (any language) ==
A metric of a video's popularity is the "Billion View Club", denoting videos which have succeeded in reaching over 1 billion views since their initial upload. Released on 30 June 2017, "Mi Gente" became the first music video by a French artist to reach one billion views, although this version of the song is not in French. Only four French-language videos, "Dernière Danse", "Papaoutai", "Ego" and "Djadja have hit the 1 billion view mark, the most recent occurring on 24 February 2025.
