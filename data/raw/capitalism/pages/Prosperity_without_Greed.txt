Prosperity without Greed: How to Save Ourselves from Capitalism (German: Reichtum ohne Gier. Wie wir uns vor dem Kapitalismus retten) is a 2016 book by the German politician Sahra Wagenknecht. The book blames capitalism for stifling innovation and not rewarding performance, and promotes small-scale economic structures as means for sustainability, competition and functioning markets.
