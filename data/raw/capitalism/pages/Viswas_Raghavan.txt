Viswas "Vis" Raghavan is Executive Vice Chairman and Head of Banking at Citigroup. Prior to his current role, he co-led investment banking with Jim Casey since 2020 and later served briefly as sole head of global investment banking at JPMorgan Chase.


== Career ==
Raghavan joined J.P. Morgan in 2000 from Lehman Brothers, where he had overseen equity-linked and derivatives capital markets for Europe and Asia.
At J.P. Morgan, he held several senior positions, including head of International Capital Markets and global head of Equity Capital Markets.
Raghavan grew up in India and studied physics at the University of Bombay. Then, he earned a postgraduate degree in electronic engineering and computer science from Aston University in the United Kingdom.
