Hamburgische Investitions- und Förderbank (IFB Hamburg) is the regional promotional bank (German: Förderbank) for the German state of Hamburg. It was established in 2005.
Together with other Förderbanken and the more commercially oriented Landesbanken, IFB Hamburg is a member of the Association of German Public Banks (VÖB).
