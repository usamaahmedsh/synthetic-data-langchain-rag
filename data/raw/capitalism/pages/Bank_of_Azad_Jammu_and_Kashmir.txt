Bank of Azad Jammu & Kashmir is the state-owned bank of Government of Azad Kashmir  which is based in Muzaffarabad, Azad Kashmir. It was founded in 2005. 
BAJK has a network of 84 branches.


== Assets ==
The total assets of the bank reached Rs. 28.4 billion (US$98 million) in the first six months of the 2022 calendar year.
