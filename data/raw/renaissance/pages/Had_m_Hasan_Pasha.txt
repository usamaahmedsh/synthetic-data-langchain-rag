Hadım Hasan Pasha (Ottoman Turkish: خادم حسن پاشا; died 1598 in Constantinople) was an Ottoman statesman. He was an Albanian Grand Vizier of the Ottoman Empire from 1597 to 1598. He was also the Ottoman governor of Egypt from 1580 to 1583.
