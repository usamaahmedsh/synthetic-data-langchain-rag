Renee Price is an American politician from North Carolina. She is a Democratic member of the North Carolina House of Representatives, who has represented the 50th district (including all of Caswell County as well as portions of Orange County) since 2023. Price previously served as a member of the Orange County Board of Commissioners from 2012 to 2022.
