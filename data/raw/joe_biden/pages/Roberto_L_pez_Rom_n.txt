Roberto López Román is a Puerto Rican politician. He was elected a member of the House of Representatives of Puerto Rico in a 2025 special election.


== Early life and Education ==
Roberto López Román was born on March 20, 1978 in Caguas, Puerto Rico. Graduated from from high school at Colegio Católico Notre Dame in Caguas. Completed his undergraduate and graduate studies at Florida International University (FIU) with a concentration in Political Science. Currently, Roberto is in the dissertation process for his doctoral degree (Ph.D) in History.


== Political career ==
He was previously president of the Caguas municipal committee. In 2023, he was a mayoral candidate.
