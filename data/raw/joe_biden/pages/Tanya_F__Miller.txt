Tanya Felecia Miller (born October 3, 1973) is an American politician from the Georgia Democratic Party who serves as a member of the Georgia House of Representatives representing District 62.
Miller is a prosecutor and assistant district attorney in Fulton County. She endorsed the Kamala Harris 2024 presidential campaign.
Miller is a candidate in the  2026 Georgia Attorney General election.
