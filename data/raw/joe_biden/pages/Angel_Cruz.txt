Angel L. Cruz is an American politician who served as a Democratic member of the Pennsylvania House of Representatives for the 180th district from 2001-2022. Cruz was a member of the Pennsylvania Legislative Black Caucus.


== Ward leader ==
Cruz is the Ward Leader of the 7th Ward Democratic Executive Committee.
