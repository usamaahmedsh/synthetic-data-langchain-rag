Yusuf M. Yusuf is a Somali-born American politician. He has served as a member of the Maine House of Representatives since December 2024. He represents the 118th district which is located in Portland, Maine. Yusuf is Somali American. After fleeing the Somali Civil War Yusuf lived in Damascus, Syria before arriving in the United States in 2007 and settling in Seattle, Washington.
