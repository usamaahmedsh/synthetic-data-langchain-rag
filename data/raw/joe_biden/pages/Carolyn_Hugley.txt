Carolyn F. Hugley (born May 2, 1958) is a Democratic member of the Georgia House of Representatives, representing portions of Columbus since 1992. She has served as House Minority Leader since 2025, and previously served as Democratic Minority Whip from 2003 to 2018. 


== Personal life ==
Hugley is an insurance agent. She is married to Isaiah Hugley, who has served as City Manager of Columbus since 2005. They have two children, Kimberly and Isaiah, Jr.
