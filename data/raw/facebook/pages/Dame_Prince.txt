Dame×Prince, also known as DamePri (ダメプリ, Damepuri), was a Japanese otome visual novel created by NHN PlayArt. It was released in Japan on March 31, 2016, for Android and iOS devices. An anime television series adaptation produced by Studio Flad, titled Dame×Prince Anime Caravan, aired from January 10 to March 28, 2018.

== Other media ==
An anime television series adaptation produced by Studio Flad, titled Dame×Prince Anime Caravan, aired from January 10, 2018, to March 28, 2018. The opening theme is "D×D×D" by Breakerz. The anime is licensed by Sentai Filmworks and streamed on Hidive.
