Rashidat Momoh (born April 27, 2004), known mononymously  as Rashidat, is a Nigerian social media personality and lip sync creator. She rose to prominence for her lip sync videos on social media.


== Life and career ==
Rashidat Momoh was born on April 27, 2004, in Alimosho, Lagos State, Nigeria. She attended Holy Savior Secondary School in Lagos State and later graduated from the University of Lagos. Rashidat began posting content in 2023, including lip-syncing videos, modeling, and music promotions. As of July 2024, she had gained over 600,000 followers on TikTok. Rashidat appeared in Anti Abiba's short movie Silent Tears alongside Asake and Sanku, which was released in December 2024. In May 2025, she posted a video with singer Ayo Maff that gained significant attention and millions of views on TikTok.
