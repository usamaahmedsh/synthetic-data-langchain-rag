Oladele David Praise (born November 27, 2002), known online as Royalnasty, is a Nigerian TikToker, content creator, and actor. He rose to mainstream fame after a uploaded short comedy skit about couples, which quickly gained millions of views. 


== Life ==
Oladele David Praise was born on November 27, 2002, in Abuja, Nigeria. He is of Yoruba descent. Praise graduated from the University of Lagos.


== Career ==
Praise uploaded his first video on TikTok in 2024. Although the video didn't gain much traction initially, he persisted in creating content that primarily focuses on humor, everyday life, and cultural insights. In 2025, Praise uploaded a short comedy skit about couples, which marked his breakthrough into mainstream popularity, after Peller and King Kaly. The video went viral, significantly increasing his TikTok likes and establishing him as a prominent content creator. Following this success, he began creating challenges with friends, posting pranks, vlogs, and unpredictable content for his audience.
