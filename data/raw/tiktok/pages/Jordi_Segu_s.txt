== Early life and education ==
From a young age, he spoke Spanish, Catalan, English, and French. In 2002, he moved to Toulouse (France) to pursue higher education in telecommunications engineering at Paul Sabatier University. Later, in 2009, he earned a master's degree in Marketing and Communication at the Toulouse Business School.


== Career ==
He began his professional career at Novacom Services and Andorra Telecom. From 2011 to 2012, he hosted a weekly guest segment on Radio Nacional d'Andorra, discussing technology news and gadgets.
In January 2020, he started gaining attention on social media, especially on TikTok, where he surpassed one million followers in just ten months. He was one of the first Spanish-speaking content creators on TikTok to share knowledge about marketing and sales. As of now, he has more than 2.7 million followers on TikTok, 2 million on Instagram, and over 720,000 subscribers on YouTube.
