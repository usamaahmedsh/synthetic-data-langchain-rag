Christelle d'Intorni (French pronunciation: [kʁistɛl dɛ̃tɔʁni]; born 2 May 1985) is a French politician who has represented the 5th constituency of the Alpes-Maritimes department in the National Assembly since 2022. A member of the Union of the Right for the Republic (UDR), she was elected to the mayorship of Rimplas in 2014, becoming the youngest official in the department at that time, before she was reelected in 2020.
In the run-up to the 2022 The Republicans leadership election, she endorsed Éric Ciotti for the party chairmanship.
