André Bruno de Frévol de Lacoste (French pronunciation: [ɑ̃dʁe bʁyno də fʁevɔl də lakɔst]; 14 June 1775 in Pradelles – 2 February 1809 in Saragosse) was a French general of the First Empire. He was killed during the Siege of Zaragoza.


== Honours ==
Lacoste's name is engraved on the Arc de Triomphe, 38th column.
