Giovanni Haag (born 20 May 2000) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for  Ligue 2 club Red Star on loan from German club Fortuna Düsseldorf.


== Career ==
Haag made his professional debut with Nancy in a 1–0 Ligue 2 win over Red Star on 23 November 2018.
On 10 July 2023, Haag signed a two-year contract with Rodez.
On 30 August 2024, Haag moved to Fortuna Düsseldorf in Germany. On 1 September 2025, Haag returned to France and joined Red Star in Ligue 2.


== Personal life ==
Haag is of Italian descent through his mother, who died when he was 15 years old.
