Rèmy-Luc Julian Michel Auberjonois  is an American actor. He is best known for his recurring role as Mr. Albin in the television series Weeds and as Dr. Emerson on the television series Mad Men.


== Personal life ==
Auberjonois is the son of René Auberjonois and Judith Mihalyi. He is married to actress Kate Nowlin. They live in Minneapolis, Minnesota. He frequently appears at the Guthrie Theater.
