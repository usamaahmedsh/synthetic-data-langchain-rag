Enzo Bardeli (born 11 April 2001) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for  Ligue 2 club Dunkerque.


== Career ==
A youth product of Lille since the age of 10, Bardeli began his senior team with their reserves. He transferred to Dunkerque in 2021, signing his first professional contract on 2 March 2022.
