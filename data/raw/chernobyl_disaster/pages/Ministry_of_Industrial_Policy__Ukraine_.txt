The Ministry of Industrial Policy of Ukraine (Ukrainian: Міністерство промислової політики України) is a former government ministry of Ukraine; it was the main body in the system of central bodies of the executive power. It was created from the former Ministry of Economy and initially was created as Ministry of Machinebuilding, Military-Industrial Complex and Conversion in 1996 and merged with the Ministry of Economic Development and Trade in March 2014 with headquarters in Kyiv.

== History ==
Ministry of Machinebuilding, Military-Industrial Complex and Conversion: 1996 - 1997
Ministry of Industrial Policy: 1997–2000
State Committee of Industrial Policy: 2000–2001 (partially responsible to Minister of Economy)
Ministry of Industrial Policy: 2001–2011
State Agency in Management of State Corporation Rights and Property: 2011–present (some authority)
Ministry of Economical Development and Trade: 2011–present (merged)
