FC Lokomotiv-KMV Mineralnye Vody (Russian: «Локомотив‑КМВ» (Минеральные Воды)) was a Russian football team from Mineralnye Vody. It played professionally in 1986–1992, 1994–1998 and 2001. Their best result was 3rd place in the South Zone of the Russian Second Division in 2001.


== Team name history ==
1986–1997: FC Lokomotiv Mineralnye Vody
1998–2003: FC Lokomotiv-Taym Mineralnye Vody
2004–2005: FC Zheleznodorozhnik Mineralnye Vody
2006–2008: FC Lokomotiv-KMV Mineralnye Vody
