Amtsgruppe Allgemeine Wehrmachtsangelegenheiten (transl. Office Group for General Wehrmacht Affairs) was a division of the Wehrmacht High Command. In 1938–1939 it was designated the Wehrwirtschaftsstab (Military Economy Staff). Hermann Reinecke was made the leader in 1939.
