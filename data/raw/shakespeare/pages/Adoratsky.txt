Adoratsky (Russian: Адора́тский; masculine) or Adoratskaya (Адора́тская; feminine) is a Russian last name.  a Russian surname originated in clergy artificially created from the Latin word meaning respected, revered and was introduced in Russian Orthodox seminaries.  Notable people with the surname include:

Varya Adoratskaya, subject of the painting Portrait of Varya Adoratskaya by Nicolai Fechin, Russian–American painter
Vladimir Adoratsky (1878–1945), Soviet communist historian and political theorist
