The Acer beTouch E140 is a smartphone manufactured by Acer Inc. utilizing the Android 2.2 (Froyo) operating system.


== Main specifications ==
Operating System: Android 2.2
Display: 2.8-inch touch screen
Processor: 600 MHz
Wi-Fi 802.11 b / g,
Bluetooth 2.1, IR
FM-radio
Camera: 3.2MP
Battery: 1300 mAH
Weight: 115 grams
