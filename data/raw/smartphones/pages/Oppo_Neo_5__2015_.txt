The Oppo Neo 5 (2015) and Oppo Neo 5s are refreshes of the Oppo Neo 5. The key difference between the refreshed 5 and 5s models is the 4G connectivity in the 5s, which the 5 lacks. The 5 and 5s cost €70 and €160, respectively.


== Specification ==
The Oppo Neo 5 (2015) was released in August 2015. It features a 4.5-inch IPS LCD display with a resolution of 480 x 854 pixels, resulting in a pixel density of approximately 218 ppi. It is powered by a Mediatek MT6582 (28 nm) chipset, featuring a quad-core 1.3 GHz Cortex-A7 CPU and a Mali-400MP2 GPU. It runs on Android 4.4.2 (KitKat) with Oppo's ColorOS 2.
