=== Hardware ===
The Oppo R831K (Neo 3) features a 4.5-inch IPS LCD display (480 x 854 pixels). It's powered by a MediaTek MT6572 (28 nm) dual-core 1.3 GHz CPU with 1 GB RAM and 4 GB internal storage, expandable via microSD. Key hardware includes a 5 MP main camera with LED flash and a 2 MP selfie camera. It runs on a removable 1900 mAh Li-Ion battery and supports dual SIM functionality.
