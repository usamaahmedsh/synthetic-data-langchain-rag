HP Slate is a small line of HP consumer tablets and all-in-ones.


== Models ==


== HP Slate 500 ==
HP Slate 500 is an 8.9-inch Windows 7 tablet PC released in 2010 by Hewlett-Packard. It features an Intel Atom Z540 processor, 2GB RAM, 64GB SSD, and a capacitive touchscreen with stylus support.

== HP Slate 7 ==

HP Slate 7 is a 7-inch Android 4.1 tablet released in 2013. It comes with a dual-core 1.6 GHz processor, 1GB RAM, 8GB storage (expandable via microSD), and a 1024×600 pixel display.
