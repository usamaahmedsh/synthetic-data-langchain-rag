Huawei Pura 80 (stylized as HUAWEI Pura80) is a series of high-end smartphones manufactured by Huawei and released on the market in China in June 11, The international variant will launch on 10 July; it succeeds the Huawei Pura 70 series and comprises four models: the Pura 80, Pura 80 Pro, Pura 80 Pro+, and Pura 80 Ultra. The Huawei Pura 80 series is the successor to the Pura 70 series, comprising four models: the Pura 80, Pura 80 Pro, Pura 80 Pro+, and Pura 80 Ultra.The standard Pura 80 model features the Kirin 9010S 5G SoC chip, while the remaining variants utilise the HiSilicon Kirin 9020 5G SoC chip.


== Features ==


=== Software ===

The Pura 80 series features AI privacy protection, alerting others when they are being observed while the screen is active.


=== Performance ===
The Pura 80 series delivers approximately 36% improved performance compared to the Pura 70 Pro+.Control responsiveness has been improved by 47%.
The HarmonyOS version of Alipay on the Pura80 series achieves a 50% faster card swiping speed compared to the previous generation.
