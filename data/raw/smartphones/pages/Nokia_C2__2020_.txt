The Nokia C2 is a Nokia-branded entry-level smartphone by HMD Global, running the Android Go variant of Android. It was announced on March 16, 2020.


== Specifications ==
The Nokia C2 has Android 9 Pie (Go Edition) operating system with a Unisoc System-on-Chip and 1 GB of RAM. It has 16 GB of internal storage, which can be expanded with a MicroSD card, up to 64 GB. The phone has a 5.7-inch HD+ IPS LCD display.
