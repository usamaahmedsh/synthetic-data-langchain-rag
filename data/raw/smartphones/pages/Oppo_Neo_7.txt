The Oppo Neo 7 is the last phone in the Oppo Neo Series, before the series became known under different aliases, such as the Oppo A Series. The phone was available in two configurations: a 3G and a 4G model. Besides network connectivity differences, these models also had different chipsets. Oppo says the reflective back on the phone, which is made by a "special laminating process" is inspired by Chinese bronze mirrors.
