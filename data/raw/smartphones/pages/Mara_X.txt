The Mara X (stylized as Mara X) is an Android smartphone in the Maraphone series, manufactured in Rwanda. It was officially announced and released on 7 October 2019 in both Kigali, Rwanda, and Durban, South Africa, alongside the Mara Z. At launch, it retailed for approximately 120,250 Rwandan francs (US$130 at the time).


== Specifications ==


=== Hardware ===
The Mara X is powered by a MediaTek MT6739 processor, with 1 GB of RAM and 16 GB of internal storage. It has a 5.5-inch (13.97 cm) HD+ (720Ã—1440) display with Corning Gorilla Glass 5. It includes a 13 MP rear camera, 5 MP front camera, fingerprint sensor, and a 3500 mAh battery. Cellular network support includes 2G, 3G, and 4G. 


=== Software ===
Both the Mara X and the Mara Z run Android One editions of Android 8.1 Oreo.
