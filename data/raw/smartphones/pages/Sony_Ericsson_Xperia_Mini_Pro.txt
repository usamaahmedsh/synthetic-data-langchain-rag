The Sony Ericsson Xperia Mini Pro (model SK17i) is an Android smartphone from Sony Ericsson, released in August 2011. The Xperia Mini Pro has a "mobile BRAVIA engine" driving a 320Ã—480 pixels 3-inch (76 mm) capacitive touch-screen, a 1 GHz Snapdragon S2 processor, a 5 megapixel camera, 512 MB of onboard RAM, a hardware keyboard and comes stock with a 2 GB microSD card (compatible with up to 32 GB).


== Overview ==
The Xperia Mini Pro runs Android 4.0, "Ice Cream Sandwich." It was touted as being an ultra-small smartphone that still retained higher-end specifications and performance.
