The Meizu M1 Note is a smartphone by Chinese manufacturer Meizu. It was announced in China on December 23, 2014, and released in early January 2015.  The Meizu M1 Note is positioned as a "quality for young" device. 


== Specifications ==


=== Hardware ===
The Meizu m1 note features a 5.5-inch 1080p IGZO IPS LCD display protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 3. It was powered by a Mediatek MT6752 octa-core processor and ran Android 4.4.4 KitKat with Flyme 4. The phone had a 13 MP main camera, a 5 MP selfie camera, and a 3140 mAh non-removable battery.
