The Samsung Galaxy A25 5G is a mid-range Android smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Samsung Electronics as a part of its Galaxy A series. It was announced on 11 December 2023 and was released along with the Samsung Galaxy A15 5 days later.
It is Samsung's A2x smartphone of 2024 that sits in between the A15 and A35. Starting from this release, only a 5G model would be released with its predecessor being the last A2x model to be released with 4G connectivity only.


== Design ==
The display is made of Corning Gorilla Glass 5. The back panel and side are made of plastic. The design of the Galaxy A25 5G looks almost similar to its predecessor, but the rear panel is now completely flat, and the transition between the rear panel and the camera unit is smoother. Also, Galaxy A25 5G is the first A2x phone that has the IP67 standard.
There is a USB-C connector, speaker and microphone on the bottom and, depending on the version, slot for 1 SIM card and microSD memory card up to 1 TB or hybrid slot for 2 SIM cards. The second microphone is located on top.  On the right side are the volume buttons and the smartphone lock button combined with a fingerprint sensor. It is also the last A2x device to include a 3.5mm headset jack.
The smartphone is sold in 4 colors, Blue Black, Blue, Light Blue and Yellow.


== Specifications ==


=== Hardware ===
The Galaxy A25 5G is a smartphone with a slate-type factor form, which is 162.1 × 77.6 × 8.3 mm in size and weighs 195 grams.
The device is equipped with GSM, HSPA, LTE and 5G connectivity, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac dual-band with Bluetooth 5 Wi-Fi Direct support and hotspot support. 1 with A2DP and LE, GPS with BeiDou, Galileo, GLONASS and QZSS and NFC.
It has a 6.5 inch diagonal notched display, Super AMOLED type, rounded corners and a resolution of 1080 × 2340 pixels, with a maximum refresh rate of 120 Hz.
The A25 has a 5000mAh non-removable battery, with support for fast charging at 25 W.
The chipset uses the Samsung Exynos 1280, which was first used on the Galaxy A53 5G. Internal storage of the device is 128 GB expandable with a microSD card up to 1 TB, while the RAM varies from 4 to 8 GB.
The rear camera has a 50 MP main sensor with an f/1.8 opening. The main camera is equipped with a PDAF, OIS, HDR mode and flash LED, while the front camera is a single 13 MP sensor.


=== Software ===
The phone was first released with Android 14 and One UI 6.0. Samsung has promised 4 years of major Android OS and One UI updates and 1 additional year of security updates for a total of 5 years worth of updates to the Galaxy A25 5G (with support ending within 2029).
