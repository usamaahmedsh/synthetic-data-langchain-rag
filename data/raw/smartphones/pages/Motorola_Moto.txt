Motorola Moto is a range of Android mobile devices manufactured by Motorola Mobility, a subsidiary of Lenovo since 2014. This article lists the various Moto series of devices as well as discontinued series.
The Moto brand is also used and marketed for certain public-safety products from Motorola Solutions and by Motorola brand licensees on certain home products and mobile accessories.


== Moto E ==
Moto E is low-end.


== Moto G ==

Moto G is low-end to mid-range. The brand was developed to target consumers with lower income levels, particularly in developing countries. Moto G was a full-function but modestly priced smartphone, priced as low as 153 euros and introduced primarily for emerging markets such as Brazil, India, and the Middle East. The Moto G's low cost also made it appealing to cost-conscious consumers in developed markets like the United States and Europe.
