Agger is a Danish surname. Notable people with the surname include:

Alma Agger, Danish singer
Daniel Agger (born 1984), Danish footballer
Gunhild Moltesen Agger (born 1945), Danish academic
Harald Agger (1889–1954), Danish hammer thrower
Knud Agger (1895–1973), Danish painter
Mads Agger (born 1999), Danish footballer
Margrethe Agger (born 1943), Danish textile artist
Nicolaj Agger (born 1988), Danish footballer
Poul Agger, Danish sprint canoeist
