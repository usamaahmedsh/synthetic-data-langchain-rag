Abdulin (Russian: Абду́лин) is a masculine surname, commonly found in Azerbaijani, Russian, and Central Asian languages. It is a variant of Abdulayev. Abdulina (Абду́лина) is the feminine surname counterpart. Notable people with the surname include:

Denis Abdulin (born 1985), Russian professional ice hockey forward
Mansur Abdulin (1923–2007), Russian memoirist
Rinat Abdulin (born 1982), Kazakhstani association football player
