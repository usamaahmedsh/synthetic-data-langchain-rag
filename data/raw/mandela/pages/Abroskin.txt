Abroskin (Russian: Абро́скин or Абро́ськин; masculine) or Abroskina (Абро́скина or Абро́ськина; feminine) is a Russian last name, a variant of Abrosimov. The following people bear this last name:

Aleksandr Abroskin (b. 1987), Russian football player
