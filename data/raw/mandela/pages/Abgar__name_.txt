Abgar (Syriac: ܐܲܒ݂ܓܲܪ) is a masculine given name of Classical Syriac origin. The name is derived from the term "Abgara" (Classical Syriac: ܐܒܓܪܐ, romanized: ʾaḇgārā, lit. 'lame') and is typically used as a given name (such as in Assyrian Neo-Aramaic). The name may also be used as a surname.
