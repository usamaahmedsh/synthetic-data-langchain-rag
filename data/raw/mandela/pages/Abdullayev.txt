Abdullayev (Russian: Абдулла́ев) is a surname, found in Azerbaijan, Russia, and Central Asia. The feminine form is Abdullayeva (Russian: Абдулла́ева). It is slavicized patronymic surname derived from the given name Abdullah. 
Variants are: Abdullaev, Abdulayev, and Abdulloev.
