Abramczyk (also rendered as Abramchuk, Abramcyk, Abramchik, Abramczik, and Abramchyk) is a Slavic surname of distant Jewish origin, most predominantly coming from Poland, and nowadays met mainly among Polish Roman Catholics. It is a patronymic surname derived from a Hebrew name 'Abram' – the original name of the biblical figure Abraham.
