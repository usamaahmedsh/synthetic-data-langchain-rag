The 2012 National Games of Nepal, is held in Mahendranagar & Dhangadi, Far-Western Region.


== Venues ==
Dhangadi Rangasala
Mahendranagar Stadium


== Participating teams ==
Teams are from all 5 Regions, four department sides Nepal Army, Nepal Police Club,Nepal A.P.F. Club & Liberation Army & University Team  participated in the this edition of National Games of Nepal.
