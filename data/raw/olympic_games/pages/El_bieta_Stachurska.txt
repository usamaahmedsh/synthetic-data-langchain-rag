Elżbieta Stachurska–Woźniak (born 24 March 1959) is a Polish sprinter. She competed in the women's 100 metres at the 1980 Summer Olympics.


== Personal bests ==
Outdoor

100 metres – 11.36 (1980)
200 metres – 23.11 (1980)
