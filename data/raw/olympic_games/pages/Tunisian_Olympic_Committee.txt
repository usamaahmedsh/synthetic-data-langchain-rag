The Tunisian Olympic Committee (IOC code: TUN; Arabic: اللجنة الوطنية الأولمبية التونسية) is the National Olympic Committee that represents Tunisia in the Olympic Movement. It was founded and recognized by the IOC in 1957. It is based in Tunis. It is member of the International Olympic Committee (IOC), the Association of National Olympic Committees of Africa and others international sports organisation.
