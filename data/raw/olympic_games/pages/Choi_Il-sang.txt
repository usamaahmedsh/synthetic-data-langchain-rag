Choi Il-sang (Korean: 최일상; RR: Choe Ilsang, born 15 July 1975) is a South Korean retired para table tennis player. He won a silver medal at the 2012 Summer Paralympics and a gold at the 2016 Summer Paralympics, both in the Class 4–5 team event.
His disability is congenital. His coach was Choi Kyoung-sik.
