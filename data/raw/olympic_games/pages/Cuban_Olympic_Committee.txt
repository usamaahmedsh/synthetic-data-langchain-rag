The Cuban Olympic Committee (Spanish: Comité Olímpico Cubano; IOC Code: CUB) is the organization that represents Cuban athletes in the International Olympic Committee (IOC), the Pan American Games and the Central American and Caribbean Games. It was created in Havana in 1926; however, it was not formally recognized by the IOC until 1955. The organization is currently directed by  José Fernández Álvarez.
The Cuban Olympic Committee is headquartered in Havana, Cuba.
