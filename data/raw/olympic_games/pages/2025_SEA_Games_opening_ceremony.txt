The opening ceremony of the 2025 SEA Games will take place on 9 December 2025 across Bangkok, beginning at 19:00 ICT (12:00 UTC). The Games will be formally opened by the king of Thailand, Vajiralongkorn. The ceremony will mark the centenary of the 1975 Southeast Asian Peninsular Games.
