The Panama Olympic Committee (Spanish: Comité Olímpico de Panamá) (IOC Code: PAN) is the National Olympic Committee representing Panama. It is also the body responsible for Panama's representation at the Olympic Games.


== History ==
Comité Olímpico de Panamá was founded in 1934 and recognised by the International Olympic Committee in 1947.
