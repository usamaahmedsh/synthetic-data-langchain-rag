Nexus AI is a generative artificial intelligence assistant platform developed by Verbosec. It includes a range of tools intended to support tasks such as writing, research, design and communication.


== History ==
Nexus AI was launched on October 25th, 2024, by Verbosec. The platform was developed to meet demand for accessible AI-based tools and is built using models from OpenAI through a partnership with Microsoft.
