Krikey AI is a San Francisco-based artificial intelligence (AI) animation company that converts videos to 3D character animations using generative AI. It also has a browser-based video editor with voice AI dialogue that uses ElevenLabs and Amazon Polly, a Canva app, and an Adobe Express Add-On.


== History ==
The company was founded by sisters Jhanvi Shriram and Ketaki Shriram in 2017 as an augmented reality gaming company after the former worked at YouTube and the latter at Google and Facebook. In 2018 and 2019, Krikey AI released an augmented reality game with the Audubon Society, one with the Ellen Fund, and one with Conservation International.
The company's Series A funding round was led by Jio Platforms with the release of a game called YAATRA, and as of 2020, Krikey AI had raised $22 million.
