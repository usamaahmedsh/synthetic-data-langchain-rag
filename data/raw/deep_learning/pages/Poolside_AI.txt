Poolside AI or poolside is an American and French startup specializing in the development of computer programs using artificial intelligence. It focuses on creating advanced AI models to automate and improve coding processes, emphasizing security, privacy, and efficiency for businesses.


== History ==
Poolside AI was founded on July 10, 2023. Its CEO, Jason Warner, is the former CTO of GitHub. It secured a seed funding of $26 million, followed shortly by an additional $100 million funding round with participation from Bpifrance, Xavier Niel, and Rodolphe Saad√©.
Founded in the United States, the company moved their headquarters to Paris in August 2024.
In October 2024, the company raised $500 million from Bain Capital, along with a pool of investors including eBay and Nvidia. This valued the company at $3 billion, giving it unicorn status.


== Activity ==
Poolside AI aims to generate computer code using a large language model.
In December 2024, Poolside and AWS announced a strategic partnership to offer secure and customizable generative AI solutions for software development on Amazon Bedrock and Amazon EC2. This collaboration allows businesses to deploy Poolside's AI Assistant and its foundational models (Malibu and Point) within their existing AWS environment, benefiting from AWS's security and performance.
