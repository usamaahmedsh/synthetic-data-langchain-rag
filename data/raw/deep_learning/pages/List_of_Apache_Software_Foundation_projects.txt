This list of Apache Software Foundation projects includes the software development initiatives maintained by the Apache Software Foundation (ASF).
In addition to active projects, the ASF maintains several related areas:

Incubator: for projects that are in the process of becoming full ASF projects.
Attic: for ASF projects that have been formally retired.
INFRA (Apache Infrastructure Team): responsible for managing and providing the technical infrastructure and services used by all Apache projects.
