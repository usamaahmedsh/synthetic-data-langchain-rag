Société du pipeline sud-européen (SPSE) is an oil pipeline company established in France.  It is an owner and operator of the South European Pipeline, a crude oil pipeline system which runs from Fos-sur-Mer in France to Karlsruhe in Germany.  The company also runs a maritime terminal in Fos-sur-mer which is made up of 40 tanks having a total capacity of 2.26 million cubic metres (80×10^6 cu ft).
The company was founded in 1958 by sixteen oil companies to build and operate the South European Pipeline. As of 2011, the shareholders are:

Total S.A. (27.84%)
ExxonMobil (22%)
Société de Participations dans l'Industrie et le Transport du Pétrole (15.4%)
BP (12.1%)
Royal Dutch Shell (10.32%)
BASF (10%)
Phillips 66 (2%)
