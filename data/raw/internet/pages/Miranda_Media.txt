Miranda Media (Russian: Миранда-Медиа) is the Internet provider of the Republic of Crimea and the federal city of Sevastopol, Luhansk and Donetsk. It is headquartered in Simferopol in Ukraine.
Russian Internet providers such as Miranda Media are subject to Russian Internet monitoring and censorship.
There have been multiple reports of cyberwarfare incidents that have involved the Miranda Media network
