Raheem Aishat Olabisi professionally known Yomidun is Nigerian social media personality, social media influencer and model.


== Early life and education ==
Yomidun is born and raised in the capital of Oyo State.


== Career ==
She started her career as a fashion model and later became a social media personality and influencer.


== Ambassadorial deals ==
Yomidun as a social media influencer has had brand ambassador deals with the following brands, A 15 million Naira ambassador deal with lifestyle brand Platnova, Tecno Mobile smartphone launched in 2024, Australian Fashion brand BabyBoo and Fashion Nova.
