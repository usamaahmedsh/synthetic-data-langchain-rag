Gujarati Wikipedia (abbreviated gu-WP) is the Gujarati language version of Wikipedia, an online encyclopedia. It was founded in July, 2004. It has 30,726 articles. As of December 2025, it generates 3 million monthly views.

As of October 4, 2022, it contains 29,986 articles and has 70,004 contributors, including 73 active contributors and 3 administrators.
As of December 2025, it contains 30,726 articles and has 83,898 contributors, including 138 in the past month and 3 administrators.
