Calon Sarjana (lit. Bachelor's Candidate) is an Indonesian YouTube channel, managed by Andre, Jennie, and Revi. Their channel associated with the Infia network.
At the time of its closure, the channel had over 13 million subscribers. As a substitute for Calon Sarjana, the manager from the channel uses Calon Ilmuwan instead of Calon Sarjana. However, in October 2024, Calon Sarjana's main channel was officially restored.
