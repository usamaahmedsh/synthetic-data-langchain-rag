Trump Unyielding: The Quest for America’s 47th Presidency is a 2024 biographical book about Donald J. Trump written by Newton Isaac. The book was published by Bizmart Holdings LLC. It was released on November 6, 2024 shortly after Trump's election into office as the 47th President of the United States. The book presents a broad account of Trump’s life and career, tracing his background from his birth and family upbringing in Queens, his early involvement in real estate and business ventures, and his emergence as a public figure through media and television.  It documents Trump’s political ascent, including his first term as the 45th President of the United States, his post-presidential activity following the 2020 election, and his return to national office in 2024. 


== Summary ==
Trump Unyielding comprises 14 chapters across 414 pages, chronicling Donald Trump's life from his childhood in Queens to his business achievements and political career. It covers key events from his birth in Queens to his inheritance of the Trump business empire, and his rise as a real estate mogul, diversification into other sectors, financial recovery after bankruptcies, and transition into politics. The narrative follows his election as the 45th president, subsequent loss to Biden, and claims of operating as a “shadow president” in Florida White Housebefore his eventual return as the 47th President.
The book also discusses two assassination attempts during his campaign and analyses Trump's alliance with public figures such Elon Musk. It concludes with an overview of his literary legacy and honors received over five decades.
The cover features a photograph of Trump taken after an assassination attempt in Pennsylvania on July 13, 2024


== Reception ==
The book was published in several formats, including eBook, audiobook, hardcover, and paperback editions. Upon release, the book appeared on Amazon’s category-based bestseller lists, with the hardcover edition ranking in its respective political biography category. The eBook version was also listed among the top new releases in the International Relations section.
News coverage in the Daily Monitor and other regional media outlets highlighted the book’s exploration of contentious political topics and contemporary campaign events. The article noted the book’s “unflinching narrative” and positioned it as “one of the most daring political biographies of the 2024 election cycle.
The book has received attention from a range of international media platforms, where it has been discussed within broader political and public-interest discourse. Coverage from multiple regions, including the Middle East, parts of Africa, and South Africa, reflects its growing visibility and engagement across diverse audiences. Media commentary generally situates the work within ongoing conversations about political leadership, governance, and contemporary developments.
