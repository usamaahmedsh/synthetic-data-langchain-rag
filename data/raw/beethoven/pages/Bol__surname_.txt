Bol is a surname in the Low Countries and in South Sudan.


== Dutch surname ==
Bol (Dutch pronunciation: [bɔl] ) is a Dutch surname with a variety of origins. These can be patronymic (after Bolle/Bole short forms of the Germanic personal name Baldo), occupational (bol = bread roll, referring to a baker), and descriptive (someone with a bol hoofd = round/bald head).
