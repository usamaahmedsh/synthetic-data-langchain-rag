Reynders or Reijnders is a Dutch patronymic surname common in the Limburg area.[1][2] The form Reinders is more common in the Eastern Netherlands and the flanking regions of Germany.[3] The Dutch given name Reinder is a variation on Reinier (from Saint Rainier) or sometimes Reinhard.[4]  Notable people with the surname include:
