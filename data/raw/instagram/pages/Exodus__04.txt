"Exodus '04" is the 3rd English single (18th overall) by Utada, written the artist  and Timbaland. It was released on June 21, 2005. It hit #24 on the Billboard Hot Dance/Club Airplay chart. Similarly to that of "Devil Inside," no music video was produced.
"Exodus '04 (JJ Flores Double J Radio Mix)"was featured on Utada's first English compilation album, Utada the Best.
