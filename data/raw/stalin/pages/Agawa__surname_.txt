Agawa (written: 阿川) is a Japanese surname. Notable people with the surname include:

Hiroyuki Agawa (阿川 弘之; 1920–2015), Japanese writer
Kouko Agawa (阿川好子), Japanese manga illustrator
Naoyuki Agawa (阿川 尚之; born 1951), Japanese lawyer, diplomat, academic and writer
Sawako Agawa (阿川 佐和子; born 1953), Japanese writer and television personality
