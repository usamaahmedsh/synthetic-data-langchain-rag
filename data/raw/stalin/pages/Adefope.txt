Adéfọpẹ́ is a Nigerian surname of Yoruba origin, typically given to males. It means “The crown or royalty shows gratitude".  This distinctive and culturally rich name symbolizes a legacy of dignity and gratitude.


== Notable individuals with the name ==
Henry Adefope (1926–2012), Nigerian politician
Lolly Adefope (born 1990), English stand-up comedian and actress
